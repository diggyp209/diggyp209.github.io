Recipe #1

  • Time: 24 mins
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
cup of butter 1
cup of sugar 1
cup of brown sugar 1
eggs 2
tea spoon vanilla extract 2
tea spoon of baking soda 1
tea spoons hot water 2
tea spoon of salt 1/2
cups of flour 3
cups of choclate chips 2
cup of walnuts 1

Step 1: put ingredients into larg mixing bowl.
Step 2:mix with mixer on high for 2 mins.
Step 3:roll dough into 2 inch ball & place on cookie sheet.
Step 4:bake in oven at 350 for 18-24 mins.
